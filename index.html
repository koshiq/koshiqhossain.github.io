Mind Controlled 3D Printed Robotic Hand

During the summer before senior year, I got a full scholarship to attend BlueStamp Engineering. The program offers students to create a project of their choice in six weeks. I had no prior knowledge in engineering, yet I managed to create a mind controlled 3D Printed Robotic Hand. The first three weeks I created a 3D Printed Robotic Hand that mimics an actual hand. Later, I realized that this device had no use if a person didn't have a hand, so I maid it mind controlled. The website isn't fully updated, and I have to put a demo video of the mind controlled aspect.
